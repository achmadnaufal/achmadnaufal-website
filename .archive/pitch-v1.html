About

I'm Naufal, a data analyst based in Indonesia. For the past several years I've built analytics and monitoring systems for pharma and environmental programs across Asia.

The first half of my career was in pharma commercial analytics, building regional sales and supply-chain dashboards. More recently I've focused on nature-based solutions, working with field teams in Indonesia, Thailand, and beyond to turn messy KoboToolbox data into decision-grade monitoring.

Stack: Power BI (advanced DAX), Python (pandas, Streamlit, geopandas), SQL, QGIS, KoboToolbox, SharePoint, Power Automate.

Education: Master's in Technology Management (Business Analytics) and Bachelor's in Statistics, Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS).
